shadows and Outlines in CSS

  1. shadows:
    1. box shadow:

      used to give the shadow to a box
      syntax:: box-shadow: h-offset v-offset blur spread color inset;

      example:

      i am a box made by the div.
      for making shadows inside use inset.

      syntax:: box-shadow: inset 15px 13px 7px 5px black;

      i am a box made by the div.


    2. text shadow:

      Used to give the shadow to text

      syntax:: text-shadow: h-offset v-offset blur color;

      example:

      Lorem ipsum dolor sit, amet consectetur adipisicing elit. Voluptates temporibus ipsum inventore maxime, pariatur tempore quisquam voluptatem praesentium rem quasi rerum eveniet nam.

  2. Outlines:

    outline follows the border it appears outside the border and the curvature of the outline follows the the border.

    its syntax is also like the border.

    syntax:: outline: 2px solid red

    EXAMPLE:

    I am a div for the outline

    • outline-offset:

      gives the distance between border and outline

      syntax:: outline-offset: 15px

      I am a div for the outline
